About the Legislation Archive

The Jordan Legislation Archive is a comprehensive digital collection of Jordan's laws, resolutions, and ordinances. This platform is designed to make legal information accessible to the public, ensuring transparency and accountability.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide easy access to Jordan's legislative records, empowering citizens and officials with accurate and up-to-date legal information.

Key Features

  • Searchable Database: Quickly find specific documents using search and filter options.
  • Accessible Format: Browse documents with a user-friendly interface.
  • Real-Time Updates: Documents are updated directly from the official records.
